# SOC2 Preparation Checklist

This checklist helps prepare PulseTrakAI‚ĄĘ for SOC2 readiness.

- Access control policies and role definitions (user/admin/system)
- Logging retention policy: 90 days for application logs; 365 days for security/audit logs
- Incident response plan and runbooks (see `/soc2/incident-response.md`)
- Data deletion process: documented retention, deletion, and verification procedures
- Backup policy: hourly WAL shipping + daily full snapshots; test restores quarterly
- Vendor risk checklist: inventory of third-party services, contract review, data access controls
- Business continuity and disaster recovery plan
- Regular security training and change management (see `/soc2/security-training.md` and `/soc2/change-management.md`)

Use the templates in `/soc2` as a starting point for documentation expected by auditors.
