# Support Model & SLA

Support Tiers:
- Free → email, 72-hour response
- Pro → 24-hour email support
- Elite → live chat, 4-hour response
- Enterprise → dedicated engineer, 1-hour SLA

Escalation Policy:
1. Tier 1: Support inbox — triage
2. Tier 2: Engineering on-call
3. Tier 3: Dedicated incident lead + customer comms

Outage Communication:
- Initial acknowledgement within SLA
- Regular status updates until resolved
- Post-incident RCA and remediation plan
